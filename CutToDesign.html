The Art of Laser Cutting

Precision Craftsmanship

Our lasers cut with incredible accuracy, reaching tolerances as fine as 0.01mm, ensuring perfect fit and detail.

Handmade Assembly

Despite high-tech cutting, each piece is carefully assembled by hand, adding authenticity and artistry.

Time Intensive

Each design involves 12-20 hours of skilled labor, balancing machine precision and human touch.

Materials and Sustainability

  • Eco-Friendly Wood: We use FSC-certified birch plywood, promoting responsible forest management.
  • Safe Finishes: Water-based, non-toxic adhesives and finishes protect health and the environment.
  • Built to Last: Our models are durable, designed to preserve their beauty for decades.
  • Sustainable Packaging: Recycled and recyclable materials ensure minimal environmental impact.
